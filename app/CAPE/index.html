CAPE - Energy Audit

The cape tool provides you with five steps to increase comfort and energy efficiency in your building.

1. Measure you building
2. Balance your system
3. Adjust temperature curve
4. Check ventilation
5. Apply extra insulation
In this APP you will be able to perform the first step i.e. measure your building to find out it's condition in terms of the heating system.
